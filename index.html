About

I have always been a problem-solver and one of those whom just loves technology.

I was also the system administrator on Hallmark’s Digital Asset Management system, which contains 14+ million assets and has 6,000 employee users. As the subject matter expert on the system, I served as the first line of defense for users/clients needing assistance.

I continuously strive to know the system’s processes and functionality to its fullest and am always yearning to learn more. I am always looking for ways to improve processes, develop and implement efficiencies.

The last project I worked on was with a small diversified team tasked with developing and deploying a new web-based application. I became the user champion and developed UX solutions within the software that met the needs of each business served by the system. I also developed workflows within the software to improve efficiencies. After working with the analysts on troubleshooting the software as we implemented improvements and deployments, as well as driving the best user experience, I wanted to learn more about both the backend and front end coding. Thus, I am attending the KU Coding Bootcamp. I cant wait to see what is in store for me.
